Login info

How to log in:
Enter your assigned username into the username box and enter your assigned password into the password box, then press login
Don't have an assigned username or password? Try your AIS student ID number. If you're a parent or visitor, use your child's or consult the office.

Password help:
If you forget your password or want to change it, click here(hyperlink to password change or a popup shows up)

Why can't I log in?
Try using a different device, most devices should still allow you to log in, but if trouble persists, consult the office.

Guidelines

Be smart. Do not post images or information unrepresentative of yourself online. Do you want others to associate you with your unthoughtful username?

Stay safe. Think before you post your personal information or experience online. Would you want the world to know your home address?

Be responsible. You are given the rights to access the internet for educational purposes. Do not use the internet to visit inappropriate websites (such as pornographic, or racist).

Be kind. Do not bully others. If you help or support someone bullying another person, that's also bullying! If you or someone is being bullied, tell your parents or a teacher as soon as possible.

Be considerate. Handle other people's and the school's IT devices with care. Don't create, upload or download viruses.

Netizenship

Report abuse for any inappropriate comments or media. Do not forward such materials to others.

Give credit to intellectual property. Cite all works that you are given permission to use.

Act with integrity. Piracy is a crime! Illegally downloading software, games, videos, music or movies is a criminal offense! Finding ways to obtain the "hacked" or "cracked' version of a software is still piracy. Purchase music and media and respect their licenses. Do not download pirated software.

Always cite your sources. Using someone else's work as your own and using someone else's work without asking for permission is not respectful. Don't plagiarize!
