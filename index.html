Website project:AIS wifi

 

Client found, Mrs.Smith/AIS Middle school

My group and I chose to help create the help page(s) for the ais wifi in middle school. We have to use the design process and planning to create the page(s) for this site.

Audience

The AIS middle school students using the school wifi

Purpose

For AIS middle school students to easily learn the rules and how to access the school wifi

Objectives

  • To instruct middle school students on how they can log in
  • To inform middles school students of their code of conduct when using the AIS wifi
  • To educate middle school students on how they can make good use of technology to collaborate
  • Make existing site more user friendly
  • appeal to middle schoolers (theme)

success criteria

  1. Splash page makes all 3 categories (Logging in, Rules and Regulations, Tech Usage Expectations) equally accessible
  2. Include AIS banner
  3. "Login info" button brings users to the corresponding section of the page
  4. "Rules and Regulation" button brings users to the corresponding section of the page
  5. "Tech Usage Expectations" button brings users to the corresponding section of the page
  6. Effective placement of video
  7. A balanced/effective layout for description and pictures of steps
  8. A functional and visible "back to top" button at the end of each of the 3 sections
  9. A theme that unifies the website: color scheme
